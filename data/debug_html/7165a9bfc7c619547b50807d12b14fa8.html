CAIQUE MARTINS PEREIRA DE MOURA TERNES

Campinas, SP

I am Caique M. P. Ternes, an MD graduate from the Federal University of Santa Catarina in Florianópolis, Brazil, with a PhD in Cardiology from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul. Currently, I serve as the director of the SBR-AF Registry, focusing on clinical outcomes of atrial fibrillation patients undergoing catheter ablation. I am also a clinical trialist at PhysioSync-HF (NCT05572736). My professional interests include advancing cardiovascular care, particularly in the fields of cardiology, heart failure, and electrophysiology.
