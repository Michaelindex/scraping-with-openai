Introduction
Caique M. P. Ternes, MD, graduated from the Federal University of Santa Catarina, Brazil, and holds a PhD in Cardiology from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul. He is the director of the SBR-AF Registry, focused on clinical outcomes in atrial fibrillation after catheter ablation, and a clinical trialist at PhysioSync-HF (NCT05572736). His interests include cardiology, heart failure, and electrophysiology.
