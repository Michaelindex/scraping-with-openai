Dr. Roberto da Silva Gomes (Former member)

Ph.D. (2011) – Universidade de São Paulo – USP, São Paulo, SP, Brazil.

 
Phone Number (Lab): +55 67 3345 3578
E-mail: roberto.gomes@ndsu.edu
Fellowship modality: PNPD-CAPES (2012-2013)
 
Graduate programs served by the Dr. Gomes: 1. Chemistry
Currently, working as an Assistant Professor at the School of Pharmacy at North Dakota State University (NDSU), in the United States (2020 to present).

Roberto S. Gomes obtained his PhD in Organic Chemistry from the University of São Paulo in 2011. Afterward, he joined the group of Prof. Adilson Beatriz at the Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul as a postdoctoral assistant to design new chemical methodologies for the synthesis of biologically active compounds. In 2013, he started his independent career as an Assistant Professor at the Federal University of Grande Dourados. His research interests are focused on C–C bond activation and cross-coupling reactions. He was a Visiting Professor at Harvard University in association with Prof. E. J. Corey’s Research Group (2017-2020), and was research advisor in the Master and Doctoral degree programs in Chemistry at Institute of Chemistry of the Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul, and Federal University of Grande Dourados. Currently, working as an Assistant Professor at the School of Pharmacy at North Dakota State University (NDSU), in the United States.
