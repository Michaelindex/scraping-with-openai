PRO
MD, MPH, DipIBLM

Dr. Luiz Sella has over 15 years of experience in Lifestyle Medicine as a physician, health educator and consultant, and has served as Medical Director of several lifestyle centres and health retreats. Dr. Sella is a board-certified specialist in Endocrinology and Metabolism, and is a Member of the Endocrine Society (USA) and the Brazilian Society of Endocrinology and Metabolism. He has completed a Master of Public Health with a major in Lifestyle Medicine at Loma Linda University (USA) and is a Certified Lifestyle Medicine Physician by the International Board of Lifestyle Medicine (IBLM). Dr. Sella is a trained tobacco treatment specialist by Mayo Clinic (USA) and a Health and Wellness coach by Wellcoaches School of Coaching (USA). In addition, he has a Plant-based nutrition certificate from the Center of Nutrition Studies at Cornell University (USA) and has completed training in Functional Medicine at the Institute for Functional Medicine. Dr. Sella is currently a doctoral candidate for the Doctor of Public Health degree at Loma Linda University.
